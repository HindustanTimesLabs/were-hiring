We’re hiring!

The Hindustan Times seeks a data journalist to join its award-winning News Apps and Interactives team at the earliest possible date. We’d prefer for you to come work with us at HT’s global headquarters in New Delhi, India, but we’ll consider great candidates who want to work remotely.

To be a good fit for our team, you must be curious, energetic, and excited about making creative projects on the Internet.

We make cool stuff on the Internet.

You should have a very particular set of skills.

  • Journalism
    Experience working as a professional reporter, publishing stories regularly, and juggling daily reporting with long-term feature and enterprise work.
  • Data gathering
    Web scraping, public records requests, Tabula, etc.
  • Data analysis
    Excel, OpenRefine, R, knowledge of statistics, etc.
  • Data visualization
    Creating charts and maps, preferably interactive ones with Javascript and related libraries like D3.js and TopoJSON, but also static ones with tools like Adobe Illustrator, ai2html and QGIS.
  • Web development and design
    HTML, CSS, Javascript and related libraries like jQuery.

You don’t have to be able to tick all five of these boxes, but you should be great at at least two of these things and have a strong desire to learn the rest. We improve with every project, and we’re always helping each other get better. It's a lot of fun.

We are accepting applications on a rolling basis. Interested applicants should send a cover letter, resume/CV and links to two of your stories or projects — not necessarily published — to harry.stevens@hindustantimes.com.
